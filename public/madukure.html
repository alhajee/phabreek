Mudukare is a clothing material worn by the Fulani that gained acceptance among the Hausa in the rural and urban communities of Northern Nigeria.

Looking at how the material is woven, one may think it was done using modern, sophisticated textile technology. No, it is woven by a very simple technology that made use of few pieces of wood,iron rod and bundles of thread.
